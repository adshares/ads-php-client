### Added
Support for all block explorer commands:
 - getAccount,
 - getAccounts,
 - getBlock,
 - getBlockIds,
 - getBroadcast,
 - getMe,
 - getMessage,
 - getMessageIds,
 - getTransaction.
 
 Support for basic transactions:
 - broadcast,
 - sendMany,
 - sendOne.
